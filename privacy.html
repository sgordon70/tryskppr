Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 29, 2026

Skppr ("we", "us", or "our") operates the Skppr browser extension and the website at tryskppr.com. This page explains what information we collect, why we collect it, and how we use it.

1. What We Collect

Skppr is designed to collect as little data as possible. Here's what we do collect:

  • Interest categories you select — stored locally in your browser, never sent to our servers.
  • Skip and thumbs up/down interactions — used anonymously to improve site recommendations.
  • Email address — only if you contact us directly at hello@tryskppr.com or sign up for Skppr Pro.
  • Payment information — if you subscribe to Skppr Pro, payments are processed by Stripe. We never see or store your card details.

2. What We Don't Collect

  • We do not track which websites you visit outside of Skppr.
  • We do not sell your data to anyone.
  • We do not build advertising profiles on you.
  • We do not collect your name, address, or phone number.

3. Sponsored Content

Skppr occasionally shows sponsored cards from publisher partners. These are clearly labeled. Clicking a sponsored card may be tracked for billing purposes (to confirm a visit occurred), but no personal data about you is shared with the publisher.

4. Third-Party Services

Skppr uses the following third-party services:

  • Supabase — our database provider, used to serve the list of sites. Supabase Privacy Policy →
  • Cloudflare — used to proxy requests securely. Cloudflare Privacy Policy →
  • Stripe — used to process Pro subscription payments. Stripe Privacy Policy →

5. Cookies

Skppr uses minimal browser storage (localStorage) to remember your preferences — like your selected interest categories and skip count. This data lives only in your browser and is never transmitted to us.

6. Children's Privacy

Skppr is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

7. Your Rights

You can reset or delete all local Skppr data at any time by removing the extension from your browser. If you have a Pro account and want your data deleted, email us at hello@tryskppr.com and we'll take care of it within 7 days.

8. Changes to This Policy

If we make meaningful changes to this policy, we'll update the date at the top of this page. We're not going to bury surprises in the fine print.

9. Contact

Questions? Reach us at hello@tryskppr.com.
